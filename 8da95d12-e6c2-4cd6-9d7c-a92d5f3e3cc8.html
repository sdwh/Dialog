Hi there, I'm excited to discuss my new book with you!

Glad to hear that. What's it about?

It's a mystery novel set in a small town. Think of it as Agatha Christie meets Gilmore Girls.

Sounds interesting. What's the title?

"Murder in Maple Creek."

Nice alliteration. Who's the main character?

Her name is Veronica Vane, a retired detective who now runs a bed and breakfast in the town.

Cool. What makes her unique?

She's a bit of a foodie and is obsessed with baking pastries. She believes that sweets can solve any problem.

Haha, I like her already. Who's the victim in the story?

It's the mayor of Maple Creek. He's found dead in Veronica's garden, and she's the main suspect.

Ooh, suspenseful. Who do you think the killer is?

I can't reveal that yet, but I promise there are plenty of twists and turns in the plot.

I can't wait to read it. When do you plan on publishing?

We're aiming for a Fall release, just in time for cozy sweater weather and pumpkin spice lattes.

Perfect timing. What's your next project after this?

I have an idea for a travel memoir about my adventures in Southeast Asia.

Sounds like you have a lot of exciting writing ahead of you. Thanks for sharing!

Thanks for listening! Can't wait to work together on this project.
