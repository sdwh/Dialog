Hi there, can you help me out with the Taipei Mass Rapid Transit routes?

Of course! What do you need help with?

I'm looking for the easiest route to get to the Taipei 101 tower. Do you know which line I should take?

Yes, you'll want to take the red line and get off at the Taipei 101/World Trade Center station.

Great, thanks! And what line do I take to get to the National Palace Museum?

You'll want to take the red line to the Shilin station, and then transfer to the shuttle bus that takes you to the museum.

Got it. Is there anything else I should know about the transit system here?

Well, it's important to know that the trains don't run 24/7, so it's a good idea to check the schedule before heading out.

Thanks for the heads up! What about buying tickets?

You can either buy single ride tickets or get an EasyCard, which you can recharge and use for multiple rides.

Sounds easy enough. Is there anything else I need to know?

Just that the trains can get pretty crowded during rush hour, so it's best to avoid traveling at peak times if possible.

Good to know. Thanks so much for your help!

No problem at all. Enjoy your travels on the Taipei Mass Rapid Transit system!
