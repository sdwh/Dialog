I've been wondering, how can we improve the quality of meat for our consumers?

What about the type of animal feed? Does it matter?

Absolutely! The quality and type of animal feed plays a big role in the taste and texture of the meat.

Temperature is another important factor. When animals are stressed, the meat quality can suffer.

So how do we ensure that the animals are comfortable and healthy?

Proper ventilation and adequate space are important, as well as regular checkups with a veterinarian.
