Good morning, I heard that you’re the best taxi driver in town. Is that true?

Well, thank you for the compliment! What can I help you with today?

I need to get to Marseille, can you take me there?

Sure! That’s a long ride, but I’ve got you covered. Have you ever been to Marseille before?

No, it’s my first time there. Do you have any recommendations for places to visit?

Definitely! You should check out the Old Port, Notre-Dame de la Garde, and the Calanques. They’re all beautiful places to explore.

Sounds great! Do you have any favorite places to eat?

Oh, absolutely! You have to try the bouillabaisse at Chez Fonfon. It’s the best in town.

I’ll definitely check that out. So, how long have you been a taxi driver?

I’ve been driving for over 10 years. I love meeting new people and hearing their stories.

No way! That’s so cool. Did you ask for an autograph?

Haha, no. I wanted to be professional. But it was definitely a memorable ride.

Well, I definitely won’t forget this ride either. Thanks for the great conversation and the ride to Marseille.

My pleasure! Enjoy your time in the city.
