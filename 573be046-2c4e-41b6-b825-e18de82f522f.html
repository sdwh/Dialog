Definitely. Use an eyeshadow primer or translucent powder on your lid before applying the eyeliner. This helps prevent smudging and makes the liner last longer.

For brown eyes, warm colors like bronze or copper will make them pop. For blue eyes, choose shades with orange or brown undertones. Green eyes look great with purple, plum, and bronze shades.

That's so helpful! And what about mascara?

Make sure to wiggle the brush back and forth as you apply to evenly distribute the product. Use a lash comb to separate your lashes and prevent clumps.

Lastly, the eyelash curler. Is it really necessary?

It's not necessary, but it can make a huge difference in opening up your eyes. Before you apply mascara, use the curler for a few seconds on each eye.
