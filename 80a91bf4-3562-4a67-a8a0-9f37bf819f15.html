Have you ever tried Hallacas?

Hallacas? What is that?

It’s a traditional dish popular in Venezuela during the holidays. It's like a tamale filled with beef, pork, chicken, olives, and raisins, wrapped in plantain leaves.

Hmm, it sounds interesting. Is it spicy?

It can be, but not really. You can adjust the spice level to your liking.

Great, I can't handle too much spice. How do you eat it? With your hands or utensils?

You can eat it with your hands, but I prefer to use a fork and knife.

Is it difficult to make?

It can be a bit time-consuming, but it's not too difficult. The tricky part is wrapping it properly in the plantain leaves.

I'm intrigued. Do you have any tips on where to find the best Hallacas?

If you’re ever in Venezuela, you can find it everywhere. But there are also some restaurants and food trucks that sell it. Just look for Venezuelan cuisine.

Okay, I will definitely try it next time.

You won't regret it. I promise, Hallacas are worth it.
