Hi there, I heard that this hotel has a great indoor pool. Can you tell me more about it?

Of course! Our indoor pool is perfect for anyone looking to take a swim or just relax. We have a heated pool, a hot tub, and even a sauna!

That sounds amazing. How can I use the pool? Do I need to reserve a time?

You don't need to reserve a time, but we do ask that you sign in at the front desk before going for a swim. Also, please remember to bring a towel from your room, as we don't provide them at the pool.

The pool is open from 6 a.m. to 10 p.m. daily.

Yes, we do ask that you shower before entering the pool, as well as refrain from using any glassware or alcohol in the pool area.

No, there isn't a lifeguard on duty, so please swim at your own risk. However, we do have a phone by the pool to call for emergency assistance if needed.

You're welcome! Enjoy your swim!
