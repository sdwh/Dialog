The Overland Track is definitely one of the most challenging ones I've led. It's a 65-kilometer hike through rugged terrain and can take up to a week to complete.

There are plenty of other hikes that are just as amazing, but not as difficult.

Of course, there are always risks involved with outdoor activities. But as long as everyone follows the safety guidelines and listens to the guide, we can avoid any potential dangers.

Don't worry, we have lots of precautions in place. And the animals here are usually more afraid of us than we are of them.
