Hi, I’m heading to Manila. Can you take me there?

Sure thing, ma’am. You’re in for a treat. Manila’s an exciting city.

Great! How long have you been driving taxis for?

Oh, for a while now. I’ve lost count, but I’ve met many interesting people like you.

I see. What’s the craziest thing you’ve seen while driving here?

Oh, there was one time when I picked up a bride and groom who were still in their wedding outfits. The bride was drinking alcohol straight from the bottle in the back seat.

Wow, that’s crazy! What did you do?

I didn't do anything. I just kept driving and focused on the road ahead. It’s sometimes better to stay out of things.

That’s true. Do you listen to music while driving?

Definitely! It’s a must. I love listening to oldies like The Beatles and BeeGees.

Same here! Those are classics. Do you like to sing while driving?

No, I’m not a very good singer. I’ll leave it to the pros.

Haha, okay. What’s your favorite Filipino food?

Oh, that’s tough. I would say it’s a tie between adobo and sisig. They’re both so delicious.

I haven’t tried sisig yet, but I’ve heard it’s amazing. I’ll have to give it a try.

Yes, you definitely should. And if you need any other recommendations, just let me know. I’m happy to help.

Thank you so much. I appreciate it. You’re a very helpful driver.

Thank you, ma’am. I try my best. We’re here in Manila now. Have a great stay here!
