Hey there, B! How's it going today on the construction site?

That's great to hear. So, have you had any interesting experiences on the job recently?

No way! Did you relocate it to a safer spot?

Good thinking. We don't want any accidental frog fatalities on our watch.

Haha, exactly. And speaking of wildlife, have you noticed those seagulls that keep hanging around the site?

Cheers to that, my friend. Another day, another job well done.
