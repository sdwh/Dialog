"Yes, I have. It means to pursue a misguided or wrong course of action, right?"

"Exactly. It originated from the practice of hunting dogs barking at the base of the tree instead of the actual prey that was hiding up above. It's always fascinating to know the origins of these phrases."

"Correct! It's often used when something doesn't go as planned and you have to start again from the beginning. How about you share an idiom that you know?"
