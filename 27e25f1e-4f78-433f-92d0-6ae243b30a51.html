Hi there, can you help me with some makeup advice?

Of course! What can I help you with?

I'm not quite sure how to properly use foundation. Should I apply it before or after applying moisturizer?

It's best to apply your moisturizer first, then let it dry for a couple of minutes before applying your foundation. This will help your foundation to last longer and look smoother on your skin.

That makes sense. And what about powder? When should I use it?

Powder is great for setting your foundation in place and controlling any shine. You can use it after applying your foundation or throughout the day to touch up any areas that might need it.

What's the difference between powder and compact powder?

Compact powder is just a more portable version of loose powder. It's great for traveling or for touch-ups throughout the day.

And what about concealer? How should I use it?

Concealer is perfect for covering up any blemishes or dark under-eye circles. You can apply it under your foundation for a more natural look or over your foundation for more coverage.
