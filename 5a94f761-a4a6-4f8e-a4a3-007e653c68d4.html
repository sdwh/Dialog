Hi there, can you tell me more about the process of transporting luggage onto the plane?

Sure thing! First, passengers drop off their luggage at check-in. Then it goes through security screening before being loaded onto the plane.

I see. Do you ever encounter any odd or interesting items during the screening?

Definitely! We've seen everything from live lobsters to giant snails. It's always exciting to see what people bring with them.

Wow, that's definitely unexpected! How about fragile or valuable items, do you have any measures to ensure their safety?

Yes, we have special handling procedures for fragile and valuable items. They are usually marked with a fragile tag and handled with extra care.

That's good to know. Is there a weight limit for luggage?

Yes, the weight limit for checked luggage is usually around 23kg, but it varies depending on the airline and destination.

I see. And what happens if luggage gets lost or delayed?

If luggage gets lost or delayed, we have a system to track it down and deliver it to the passenger as soon as possible.

That sounds like a relief for passengers. Thanks for explaining the process to me!

No problem at all. Have a good flight!
