If you have time, catch a Broadway show. It's a fantastic experience! You could also visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art or the American Museum of Natural History.

Take a walk on the High Line, an elevated park built on an old railroad. It's a unique and beautiful way to see the city. Also, avoid the tourist traps in Times Square and check out some of the city's neighborhoods like SoHo or the West Village.
