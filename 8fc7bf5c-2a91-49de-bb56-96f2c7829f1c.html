Good morning sir, please have a seat. Can I get you anything to drink?

Umm, just a glass of water would be great.

Sure thing. So, can you tell me why you’re here today?

Well, I guess you caught me red-handed. I was caught shoplifting at the mall.

I see. What did you steal exactly?

Just a few small things, really. Some candy, a magazine, and a toy car.

Hmm, well, that’s not too serious. However, stealing is still a crime. Do you have an explanation as to why you did it?

Honestly, I wasn’t really thinking. I just saw the items and thought it would be easy to grab and go.

I understand, but it’s important to think before you act. Now, will you be able to pay for the items you stole?

Yes, I can pay for them. I didn’t mean for this to happen.

I’m glad to hear that. Let me just write up a citation for you to sign stating that you acknowledge the theft and will pay for the items. Do you have any questions for me?

No, not really. Just give me the fine so I can pay it and be done with this.

Alright, I’ll get that for you. Thank you for cooperating.

Thank you for being kind and understanding.

No problem, it’s all part of the job. Take care and have a good day.

You too, Officer.
