Welcome to the Melbourne Museum! Do you have any specific exhibits you’re interested in today?

Thanks for having me! I’m really interested in learning about Australian Aboriginal culture. Do you have any exhibits on that?

Absolutely! Our First Peoples exhibition showcases the traditions and history of the indigenous people of Australia. Would you like a tour guide for that?

Yes, please! That sounds perfect.

Great! Let’s head over there now. Along the way, I’ll give you some background on how the exhibition was developed and what you’ll see inside.

Sounds like a plan.

So, did you know that the connection between the Aboriginal people and the land is an integral part of their culture?

No, I had no idea. Can you tell me more?

Of course! The exhibition features a range of exhibits that highlight the Aboriginal people’s traditions, music, dance and incredible artworks, such as paintings and sculptures.

That’s really interesting. I’m excited to see it all.

You’ll also get to experience a simulated journey through the Aboriginal people’s land, life, and creation stories. It’s quite an immersive experience.

Wow, I can’t wait!

Another exhibit is a display of the different tools and weapons used by the Aboriginal people in their daily lives.

That’s really cool. Were they mostly made from natural materials?

Yes, they were. The traditional tools were made from natural materials such as sticks, rocks, and animal bones.

I’m amazed at how resourceful they were.

They absolutely were! The exhibition showcases how they used their environment to create everything they needed and how their values and beliefs guide their way of life.

That’s such an interesting way of living. It really makes me appreciate their culture.

Yes, Australian Aboriginal culture is truly fascinating and has much to teach us about our relationship with nature and each other.

Thank you so much for the tour, I’ve learned so much about the Aboriginal people and their culture.

You're welcome! I’m glad you enjoyed it. Is there anything else you’d like to see in the museum?

Yes, are there any exhibits on Australian wildlife?

Of course, the Wild exhibition showcases some of Australia's unique and fascinating wildlife. Let’s head over there next!
