Good morning, sir! How are you doing?

Good morning, ma’am! I’m doing well, thank you for asking. How about you?

I’m doing great, thanks. I’m excited to go to Manila today. Have you been there before?

Yes, I have. It’s a beautiful city with many interesting places to see. What are your plans for today?

I’m planning to visit some museums and historical sites. Do you have any recommendations?

Oh, definitely! You must visit the National Museum of Fine Arts and the Rizal Park. Both are very popular among tourists.

Sounds great. I’m also looking forward to trying some Filipino cuisine. Do you have a favorite dish?

Adobo is my favorite! It’s a savory dish made with meat, soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. You should try it!

That sounds delicious. What kind of music do Filipinos like to listen to?

Well, we love our traditional folk music, but we also love pop and rock music. Have you heard of our famous singer, Sarah Geronimo?

No, I haven’t. What kind of music does she sing?

She sings pop music and has won many awards. You should listen to her songs while you’re here.

I’ll definitely look her up. How long have you been a taxi driver?

I’ve been driving for 10 years now. It’s a great way to see the city and meet new people.

That’s wonderful. Do you have any funny stories to share?

(laughs) Oh, I have many! One time, a tourist forgot his luggage in my car and I had to chase him down the street to return it. It was quite a scene!

(laughs) That sounds funny indeed. Well, we’re almost there. Thank you for the lovely conversation and the great recommendations.

You’re welcome, ma’am. Enjoy your stay in Manila!
