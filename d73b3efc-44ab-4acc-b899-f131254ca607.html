Hi B, how are you doing today?

Hey A, I'm doing pretty well, thanks. How about you?

I'm doing well too. Today, we're going to learn a new word. Do you know what "ambitious" means?

No, I've never heard of it before. What does it mean?

"Ambitious" is an adjective that describes someone who has a strong desire or goal to achieve something great.

Oh, got it. So, like someone who wants to be a doctor or a lawyer?

Exactly! We can say that they have ambitious goals or aspirations.

I see. Can you give me an example sentence?

Sure. "Samantha is an ambitious student who works hard to get good grades and wants to go to a top university."

That makes sense. Is "ambitious" a positive or negative word?

It can be both. If someone is too ambitious and doesn't care about others, it can be negative. But generally, it's seen as a positive trait because it shows motivation and drive.

Okay, I understand. Thanks for explaining that to me, A.

You're welcome, B. Keep working hard and stay ambitious in whatever you do!
