Hi there! Have you ever designed a website before?

Yes, I actually have. Why do you ask?

I'm currently designing a website for my friend's business and I'm wondering what are some basic principles of website design. Do you have any advice?

Sure! First and foremost, your website should have a clear and easy-to-use navigation menu.

That makes sense. What about the design layout?

Your design should be visually appealing, but not too cluttered. Keep it simple and clean.

Good point. What about the content?

Your content should be well-organized and easy to read. Break it up into sections with headings and use bullet points for important information.

That's helpful. What about images and videos?

Use high-quality images and videos that are relevant to your content. But be careful not to make them too large, as they can slow down the loading time of your website.

Ah, I didn't think of that. Is there anything else I should keep in mind?

Yes, make sure your website is mobile-friendly! More and more people are browsing the web on their phones and tablets, so it's important to have a responsive design.

Great advice! Thanks so much for your help.

No problem, happy to help. Let me know if you have any other questions during the design process.
