Hi there! What are you working on today?

Oh, hi! I'm making some traditional candies from Chaozhou. Would you like to try some?

Definitely! What kind of candies are they?

They're called "meisan mian". It's made from rice flour, sugar and maltose syrup, and it has a chewy texture.

Sounds delicious! How do you make it?

First, we mix the rice flour and sugar together, and then shape it into a flat disc. We then add the maltose syrup to give it that signature chewy texture.

That seems simple enough. How long does it usually take to make a batch?

It can take up to three hours to make a batch, but it's worth it for the taste.

I can imagine. Do you have any other popular candies?

Yes, we also have "lian gao" which is glutinous rice cake filled with sweet bean paste, and "ganghuagao" which is a type of crispy candy.

Wow, you certainly have a variety of sweets! Do you sell them all year round?

Yes, we do! People love them as gifts for holidays, birthdays, and special occasions.

That's great to hear. I think I'll have to get some for my friends and family too. Thanks for the introduction!

No problem, always happy to share our traditions with others. Enjoy the candies!
