First, start with understanding the basic principles of DevOps. You can do this by reading resources and watching videos online.

There are many good resources available, including books like "The Phoenix Project" and "Continuous Delivery". As well as online courses and certifications like AWS DevOps Engineer and Google Cloud's DevOps Certification.

Another option is to attend meetups and conferences where you can hear from experts and network with others interested in DevOps.

Definitely! DevOpsDays and DevOpsCon are great conferences to attend. And for meetups, look for groups in your area on Meetup.com, such as DevOpsNYC or DevOpsLondon.
