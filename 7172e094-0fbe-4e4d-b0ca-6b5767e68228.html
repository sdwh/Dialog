Hey B, I hear you had a run-in with the law.

Yeah, I got caught red-handed stealing some candy.

You should have stuck to the old saying "trick-or-treat" instead of "trick and cheat."

Ha! I guess I was feeling a little too bad to the bone.

So, what did the officer say to you during questioning?

Well, at first I was a little nervous, but the officer was actually really friendly.

That's good to hear. What did he ask you?

He asked me why I did it and then he started cracking some jokes. We ended up chatting about our favorite candy.

Wow, sounds like you made a new friend. Did he at least let you off with a warning?

Yeah, he was a good guy. He let me off with a warning, but not before he made me promise to never steal candy again.

Well, you better stick to your promise or you'll really end up in the slammer.

You're right, it's not worth getting caught for a lousy pack of candy.

So, what lesson did you learn from all this?

I learned that crime doesn't pay. But hey, at least I got a good story out of it.

True, and maybe next time you'll think twice before committing a sweet crime.

Yeah, maybe I'll just stick to buying candy like a law-abiding citizen.
