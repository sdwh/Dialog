Hi there! So, how long have you been a taxi driver in Los Angeles?

Hi! I’ve been driving for about 10 years now. LA is a fun city to drive in!

I’m excited for this trip. I’ve never been to Los Angeles before. Have you ever been to any tourist attractions around here?

Yeah, I’ve been to a couple of places. The Hollywood Walk of Fame is pretty cool, and so is the Griffith Observatory. Have you planned out your itinerary?

Not really - just planning to explore the city! But definitely want to hit up some good restaurants for some tacos and burgers.

Oh, for sure! LA has some amazing food spots. Have you ever tried “In-N-Out” burger?

No, I haven’t. Is it really that good?

Yes, it’s a must-try! Let me know if you need more recommendations.

Thanks! Hey is it true that LA traffic is always really bad?

Oh yeah, it can definitely get pretty congested. But that’s the best time to people-watch, and you might even see a few celebrities driving around.

(laughs) That would be awesome! But honestly, I’m just excited to be here and experience everything.

That’s the right attitude to have! LA has so much to offer. (pauses for a moment) Oh no, looks like we’re stuck in some traffic already.

(sighs) Well, I’m in no rush. Maybe we can play some music to pass the time?

Good idea. Let me see what I have queued up. (turns on some music) How about some classic rock?

Perfect! Nothing like some good tunes to make the traffic less frustrating. (both laugh)

It’s moments like these that make the job worth it. Meeting new people and having fun conversations.

Absolutely. Thanks for being a great taxi driver and making this ride enjoyable.

(smiling) Happy to help! And welcome to Los Angeles.
