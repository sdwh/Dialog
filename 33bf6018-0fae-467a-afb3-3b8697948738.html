Good morning, sir. Can you please state your name and reason for being here at the station?

Hello. My name is Bob and I’m here because someone stole my bike last night.

I see. Could you please describe the bike to me? We’ll start looking for it right away.

It’s a red and black bike with a yellow seat. It’s quite new actually, only a few months old.

Thank you for the description. We’ll do our best to find it. By the way, have you ever been to the station before?

No, I haven’t. Why do you ask?

Well, you seemed pretty calm and collected for someone who just lost their bike.

Yeah, I guess. It’s just a bike. I’m more upset that it was a gift from my girlfriend.

Ah, I understand. Women can be quite particular about their gifts.

Tell me about it. I remember one time I got her a subscription to a gym and she was mad at me for months.

Gym membership? That’s risky territory. You’re better off with flowers or chocolates.

Yeah, I learned that the hard way. Thanks for the advice.

No problem. Hopefully we’ll find your bike soon and you can surprise your girlfriend with it.

That would be great. I don’t know how much longer I can keep borrowing her bike.

Borrowing her bike, huh? Well, it looks like you might be in the doghouse for a while. Good luck, Bob.

Ha! Thanks, officer. I appreciate it.
