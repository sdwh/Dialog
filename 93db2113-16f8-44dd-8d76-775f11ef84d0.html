Look at those ducks in the pond, B! Can you count how many there are?

One, two, three… twelve! There are twelve ducks!

You’re so good at counting! Do you want to feed them some bread?

Yes, please! Can we get some from the snack bar?

Sure thing. Let’s go get a bag of bread for the ducks and some ice cream for ourselves.

Yay! Ice cream!

(laughs) You always get excited for ice cream. What flavor do you want?

I want chocolate and strawberry, please!

Those are two very different flavors, but you got it. Let’s go.

Look, mom! There’s a squirrel on that tree!

(pointing) Oh, I see it. It’s eating a nut.

Can we feed the squirrel too?

We can’t feed it ice cream, but we can leave a small piece of bread for it.

Okay, can I do it?

Of course. Be gentle and leave it on the ground for the squirrel to find.

(places bread on the ground) There you go, little squirrel.

You’re such a kind soul, B. Let’s go sit on that bench and enjoy our treat.

(smiling) Okay, mom. This is the best day ever.
