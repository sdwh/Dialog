No, Officer A. Could you enlighten me?

We received a report that you were seen stealing a large amount of donuts from a local shop. Can you explain your side of the story?

Well, we have surveillance footage of you leaving the shop with a large bag. Care to explain that?

(smirks) Are you sure about that? Bagels and donuts are quite different.

(chuckles) Alright, Mr. B. We'll let you off the hook this time. But let this serve as a lesson to always check your food before leaving the store.
