Good morning, sir. Can I have your name, please?

(sighs heavily) It's John Doe.

And what brings you here today, Mr. Doe?

(nervously) I, uh, I was just at the wrong place at the wrong time, officer.

(leans forward) Can you elaborate on that, sir?

(looks away) Well, I was hanging out with some friends and things got out of hand.

(skeptical) Things got out of hand? What do you mean by that?

(hesitant) We were just having a few drinks and then someone started a fight. Before I knew it, the police were there and we got arrested.

(disapproving) So you were involved in a brawl, is that it?

(ashamed) Yeah, I guess so. I'm sorry, officer. It won't happen again.

(serious) Well, that's not up to me to decide, sir. But I hope you've learned your lesson today.

(nodding) Yes, officer. I have.

(smiling) Alright then. Stay out of trouble and have a good day, Mr. Doe.

(grinning) Thank you, officer. You too. (gets up and walks out)
