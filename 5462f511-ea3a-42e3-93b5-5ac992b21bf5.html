Hi there! How are you doing today?

I'm doing great, thank you. I'm excited to be in the art studio and working on my latest project.

Wonderful! So, what kind of artwork are you currently focused on?

I'm experimenting with mixed media pieces. I want to combine different textures, colors, and materials to create something unique.

That sounds like a lot of fun! Which materials are you thinking of using?

Well, I have some acrylic paints, paper cut-outs, and fabric scraps. I also want to add some 3D elements, like wire or feathers.

That's a great mix! Have you thought about what kind of message or feeling you want to convey with this work?

Yes, I want to express a sense of whimsy and playfulness. I want it to evoke feelings of joy and curiosity.

I love that! It's important to create art that brings positivity into the world. Are there any artists that inspire you in this vein?

Definitely! I'm a big fan of Yayoi Kusama's use of bright colors and patterns, and also the playful sculptures of Claes Oldenburg.

Both amazing artists! What do you feel is the biggest challenge of this project so far?

I hope so! Thanks so much for your encouragement and feedback, it always helps to have a fresh perspective.
