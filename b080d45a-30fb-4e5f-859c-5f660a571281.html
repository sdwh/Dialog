Hey B, have you heard of the ISO 27001 Information Security standard?

It's a standard that helps organizations keep their information secure. It covers everything from risk management to data protection.

Interesting, but why does it matter?

Well, in today's digital age, information is often the most valuable asset a company has. Without proper security measures in place, sensitive information can be easily compromised.

I see. So how does this standard work exactly?

Basically, the standard lays out a framework for identifying and managing security risks, as well as implementing and maintaining effective security controls.

That sounds pretty intense. Is it hard to implement?

It can be challenging, but the benefits of having a solid security posture far outweigh the difficulties. Plus, there are plenty of resources out there to help organizations get started.
