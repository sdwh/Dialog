Auckland and Wellington are both fantastic cities to visit. Auckland has a great waterfront area and plenty of restaurants, while Wellington is known for its arts and culture scene.

New Zealand is famous for its seafood, so definitely try some green-lipped mussels and fresh fish. Also, don't forget to try some lamb - it's a staple in our cuisine.

Yum, I can't wait to try it all. How long do you think I should spend in New Zealand?

Ideally, I would recommend at least two weeks so you have enough time to see both the North and South Islands. But if you're short on time, you can still have a fantastic trip in one week.
