“Hey B, have you ever tried Parrilla before?”

“It's a style of preparing meat that originated in South America. The meat is cooked on an open flame grill.”

“You can find anything from beef, chicken, pork, and even lamb. It's a real meat lover's paradise!”

“Definitely! You can have flavorful chimichurri sauce, grilled vegetables, and even cheese.”

“There's a great place downtown called 'Parrilla Pampas'. They have an amazing selection of meats and sides.”
