Dad, I heard that heart attacks are related to weight. Is that true?

Well, there is a correlation between weight and heart attacks. People who are overweight or obese are at a higher risk.

So, does that mean we should all be super skinny?

No, definitely not. Being underweight can also be unhealthy. The key is to maintain a healthy weight for your body type.

That makes sense. What other factors contribute to heart attacks?

Well, things like smoking, high blood pressure, and high cholesterol can also increase your risk.

Yikes, that doesn't sound good.

No, it's not. But the good news is that many of these risk factors can be managed or even prevented with a healthy lifestyle.

What do you mean by a healthy lifestyle?

Eating a balanced diet, regularly exercising, quitting smoking, and managing stress are all important for a healthy heart.

So, it's not just about weight?

That's right. It's about taking care of yourself overall.

I think I understand now. Thanks, Dad!

Anytime, kiddo. It's important to start taking care of your heart early on in life.
