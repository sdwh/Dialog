Hello! Yes, that's right. We specialize in traditional Chaozhou candies.

It's a delicate process. We use a mixture of sugar, maltose, and water to create a sticky dough, which we then shape into various designs before drying them out.

It varies, but on average it takes about 5-6 hours from start to finish.

That's dedication! What's your most popular candy?

Our peanut candy is definitely a crowd-pleaser. People love the combination of sweet and nutty flavors.
