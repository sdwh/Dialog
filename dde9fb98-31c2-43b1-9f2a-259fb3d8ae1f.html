Hey there B, ready for some beach volleyball?

Great, let’s get started then. We’ll work on your serves and spikes today.

Sure thing. First, hold the ball in your non-dominant hand and toss it up with your dominant hand. Then, make contact with the ball at the highest point with a flat hand.

Got it. And what about spiking?

Well, the key is to have a good approach. Take a few steps back, then a few forward and jump high. Aim for the sweet spot of the ball with a firm hand.

Okay, I’ll keep that in mind.

Don’t worry, you’ll get the hang of it soon enough. Just keep practicing regularly.

I will. This is such a fun way to stay active and fit.
