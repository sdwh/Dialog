Good morning, everyone. Let's start the class with a little game. Who can tell me what rhymes with "cat"?

I know, I know! It's "bat"!

Very good! Now, can you tell me something that starts with "B" and ends with "ook"?

Hmm, this is a tough one. Cook, no, that doesn't start with a "B". Oh, I got it! Book!

Excellent! You're quite the wordsmith, aren't you? Now, who here loves to read?

Me! Me! I can't get enough of books.

That's fantastic. What's your favorite book?

It's "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone". I've read it more times than I can count.

Ah, a fellow Potterhead. I love those books too. Do you know what Hogwarts House you'd be sorted into?

I think I'd be in Gryffindor because I value bravery and courage.

I like your thinking. But don't forget about the other Houses, they all have their unique qualities. Speaking of which, did you know that there's a real-life Hogwarts in Poland?

What?! Really? Can we visit it someday?

Absolutely. It's actually a castle called Zamek Czocha, and they offer tours and even have a Harry Potter-themed weekend.

That sounds amazing. I can't wait to go there and live out my Hogwarts dreams.

Well, that's all the time we have for now. See you next class, everyone. And remember, keep learning and having fun!
