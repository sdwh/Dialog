Good day, officer! Is there a problem?

Good day, sir. I noticed that you’ve been driving slowly. Can I see your ID and license, please?

Sure. Here you go.

Thank you. Ah, I see you’re from out of town. Are you aware of the road signs around here?

I’m not quite sure. Can you enlighten me?

Yes, of course. Up ahead, you’ll see a triangular sign with red border and the word YIELD in bold letters. It means that you need to give the right of way to other vehicles.

Oh, I think I’ve seen that before. Does that mean I have to stop completely?

Not necessarily. You only have to stop if there are cars coming from the other direction or if there are pedestrians crossing the road. Otherwise, you can slow down and proceed with caution.

I see. Thanks for reminding me. Is there anything else I should know?

Just make sure to always follow the traffic signs and signals. It’s for your safety and everyone else’s as well.

Understood. I’ll keep that in mind. Thank you for your time, officer.

No problem. Drive safely and have a pleasant day ahead!
