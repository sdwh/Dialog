Hi, I'm planning a trip to Lithuania and I was hoping you could help me with some sightseeing recommendations.

Of course, I'd be happy to help. Have you looked into visiting Vilnius, the capital city?

Yes, I definitely want to explore Vilnius. Any must-see sites?

The Vilnius Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and has beautiful architecture. I'd also recommend visiting the Trakai Island Castle and the Hill of Crosses.

Those sound great. What about food? Any traditional Lithuanian dishes I should try?

Definitely try Cepelinai, which are potato dumplings filled with ground meat or curd, and Kugelis, a potato pudding. For dessert, Sakotis is a traditional cake made by baking layers of batter on a rotating spit.

Oh, my mouth is watering already. Do you have any other recommendations for day trips outside of Vilnius?

Kaunas, the second-largest city in Lithuania, has a lot of historical landmarks and museums. The Curonian Spit also has beautiful sand dunes and nature reserves.

That sounds like a great itinerary. Thank you so much for your help!

My pleasure. Have a wonderful trip to Lithuania!
