Hi there! I was wondering if you could help me with the laundry service?

Absolutely! We offer laundry service for our guests. What can I do for you?

Well, I have a few items I need washed. How does the laundry service work?

It's pretty straightforward. You just need to fill out the laundry form in your room, include your laundry items and instructions, and leave it in the laundry bag provided. Then, drop it off at the front desk before 11 am, and your laundry will be returned to you by 6 pm the following day.

Great! And how much will it cost?

There is a price list in your room, but we offer different packages depending on the weight of your laundry. It starts from 10 dollars for up to 5 pounds.

That sounds good to me. Is there anything else I need to know?

Just that we offer dry cleaning services too, and the prices for that are also listed in the laundry form. Also, please note that we don't wash delicate items, but we do offer an express service for an additional fee.

Thank you so much for your help. I'll definitely be taking advantage of the laundry service before I leave.

You're welcome! Have a great stay with us.
