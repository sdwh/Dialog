Good morning, class! How was your weekend?

Hi, teacher! My weekend was great. I went hiking with my friends.

That sounds like a lot of fun. Did you see any interesting wildlife on the trail?

Yes, we saw a couple of rabbits and even a few deer!

Wow, that's amazing. What about you, John? How was your weekend?

That's awesome. What kind of music do they play?

Ah, I see. I'll have to check them out. Now, let's get started with today's lesson on adjectives. Can anyone give me an example of a positive adjective?

Happy!

Excellent. And what about a negative adjective?

Great job, John. Now let's practice using these adjectives in sentences. How about this one: "I feel happy when I'm with my friends."

"I feel sad when it rains."

Perfect. Remember that adjectives can describe feelings, people, places, and things. Let's keep practicing and have a great class today!
