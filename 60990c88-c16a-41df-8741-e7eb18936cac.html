Good evening, welcome to our cheese and wine pairing section. My name is A, I am the cheesemonger here. What kind of cheese do you fancy tonight?

Hi A, great to meet you. I’d love to try some French cheese tonight. What do you recommend?

Well, if you’re a fan of soft and creamy cheese, I suggest trying camembert or brie. If you’re into strong and sharp flavors, then Roquefort is the way to go.

Hmm…I think I’ll go for the camembert. What about wine?

Great choice! As for wine, I recommend a smooth and mellow red wine to balance the acidity of the cheese. How about a Côtes du Rhône?

Sounds good to me. What makes French cheese so special?

The secret is in the terroir, the unique environment where the cheese is made. The soil, air, and climate affect the taste and texture of the cheese.

That makes sense. What are some other popular French cheese and wine pairings?

For a light and refreshing cheese, try pairing goat cheese with Sancerre. For a bold and earthy cheese, try pairing Comté with a full-bodied Bordeaux.

Wow, I’ve learned so much from you! How do you become a cheesemonger?

It’s a long and delicious journey. I started as a cheese lover, then became an apprentice in a cheese shop, and finally studied at a cheese academy.

That’s impressive. Do you have a favorite cheese?

As a cheesemonger, I love all cheese equally. But if I have to choose, I’d say a perfectly ripe Brillat-Savarin.

You really know your cheese! Thank you for the wonderful recommendations.

My pleasure. Thank you for coming and enjoy your cheese and wine pairing!
