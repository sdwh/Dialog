Hi there, can you help me with the laundry service?

Of course, I’d be happy to help. Do you have any laundry that needs washing?

Yes, I have some clothes that need to be laundered, but I’m not sure how the process works.

It’s quite simple, you can fill out the laundry request form in your room and indicate the items that need washing. Our staff will collect them and return them to you, cleaned and pressed.

That sounds great! How long does it usually take?

Our laundry service usually takes 24 hours, but if you need your clothes back sooner, we offer express service for an additional fee.

That’s good to know. And what about the prices?

Our prices are competitive and based on the weight of the laundry. You can find the prices in the laundry bag in your room.

Thanks for the information. One more question, how do I pay for the service?

You can charge it to your room account and settle the bill when you check out.

Perfect! Thanks for your help.

You’re welcome. Don’t hesitate to let us know if you have any other questions or concerns. Enjoy your stay!
