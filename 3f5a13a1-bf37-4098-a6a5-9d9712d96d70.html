Good morning, driver. My name’s A, and I’m headed to Delhi for a business meeting. How are you today?

I’m doing great, thanks for asking. It’s a beautiful day outside, isn’t it?

Yes, it is. I’m glad that it’s not raining like it was yesterday.

Yeah, the rain can be pretty tough on the traffic here. But I’m used to it. Have you ever been to Delhi before?

No, it’s my first time. I’m excited to explore the city and try the local cuisine.

Oh, you’re in for a treat! Delhi has amazing street food, especially the chaat and kebabs.

That sounds delicious. By the way, your car is very clean and comfortable. Do you take good care of it?

Yes, I do. I’ve been a taxi driver for over 10 years, and I know that a well-maintained car helps me provide better service to my customers.

That’s smart. Speaking of which, how long do you think it will take to get to Delhi?

It depends on the traffic, but normally it takes about 5 hours from here. But don’t worry, I’ll make sure we get there safely and on time.

I appreciate that. Do you listen to music while you drive?

Of course! Music helps me stay calm and focused on the road. Would you like to hear some Indian music?

Sure, why not? I’d love to learn more about the local culture.

Great! I’ll put on some Bollywood hits for you. If you like them, I can recommend some Indian movies for you to watch during your stay.

That sounds like a fun way to pass the time. Thank you, driver. You’re a helpful assistant.

It’s my pleasure, A. It’s always nice to have a pleasant conversation with my passengers. We’ll be in Delhi before you know it.
