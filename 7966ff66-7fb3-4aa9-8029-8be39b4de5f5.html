Hi, B! This beach is such a great spot for brainstorming. I've got an idea for a novel and I'd like to get your thoughts on it.

Sounds like a plan! What's your idea?

Well, it's about a group of stranded tourists on a deserted island who are forced to work together to survive.

That's a classic plotline but it still has potential. What's the twist?

The twist is that one of the stranded tourists is a famous actor who's been living a double life. His true identity is revealed as the story progresses.

Interesting! Do you have any other characters in mind?

Yes, there's a young couple who are on the brink of a breakup and being stranded tests their relationship. And there's also an elderly woman who becomes the moral compass of the group.

This all sounds great! So what are you thinking for the title?

"Castaways and Controversies"?

Hmm, that's a bit too serious. How about "Survival of the Silliest"?

(laughs) I love it! That's perfect.
