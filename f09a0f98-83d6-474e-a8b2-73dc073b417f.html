How about "the early bird gets the worm"?

Great example! Do you know what that means?

It means that if you wake up early and start your day, you'll have a better chance at success.

Excellent! Now, let's see if you can guess the meanings of these two idioms: "cats and dogs" and "raining cats and dogs."

Very close! "Cats and dogs" means a really heavy rain, and "raining cats and dogs" means it's raining really hard.
