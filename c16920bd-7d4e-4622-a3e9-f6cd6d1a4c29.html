You can take the Tokyo Metro Marunouchi line from Shinjuku to Otemachi Station, then transfer to the Toei Asakusa line to get to Asakusa.

The JR Yamanote line runs every 3-5 minutes during peak hours and every 10-15 minutes during off-peak hours. The Tokyo Metro Marunouchi line runs every 3-4 minutes during peak hours and every 6-9 minutes during off-peak hours.
