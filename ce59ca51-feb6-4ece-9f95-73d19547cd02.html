Have you ever tried Gado-gado?

Gado-what?

Gado-gado. It’s an Indonesian dish.

Hmm, I haven’t tried anything Indonesian before. How does it taste?

It’s like a salad, but with peanut sauce. It’s rich and nutty, but not overpowering.

That sounds interesting. How do you eat it?

You mix all the vegetables with the sauce and then eat it with tofu, tempeh, and boiled egg.

Sounds like a healthy option.

Definitely. And it’s also vegetarian, so a good choice if you’re trying to cut down on meat.

I’m sold. Let’s go try it!

You won’t regret it. And be sure to save some sauce for later. It’s addictively good.

I can’t wait. Do you know any good Indonesian restaurants around here?

There’s one I know that does amazing Gado-gado, but be warned: the spice level is not for the faint-hearted.

Bring it on! I can handle the heat.

I’m excited to share this culinary adventure with you.

Same here. I’m glad we share the same food philosophy – life is too short to not try new things!
