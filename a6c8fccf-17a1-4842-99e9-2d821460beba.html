Hi there, I’m a tourist here in this beautiful beach. My name is Linda. Nice to meet you!

Hi Linda, I’m Nick, a tour guide in this area. It’s my pleasure to meet you too. What do you want to know about our marine conservation efforts here?

Well, I’m very impressed with the clear water and amazing marine life here. How do you make sure that this place stays this way?

We have several programs in place, such as limiting the number of boats that can anchor here and conducting regular beach clean-ups. We also educate tourists about responsible waste disposal.

That’s amazing! Do you know what kinds of marine species live in this area?

Yes, there are different kinds of fish, clams, crabs, and even sea turtles. Have you tried snorkeling here?

Not yet, but I definitely want to! Can I see sea turtles while snorkeling?

Definitely! They are often spotted swimming in the shallow waters. Just remember not to touch them or disturb their habitat.

Got it. I’ll keep that in mind. By the way, do you know of any coral reefs nearby?

Yes, we have a few coral reefs that are off-limits to tourists to protect them from harm. But we can still appreciate them from a distance.

I see. It’s great to know that there are efforts to preserve the marine ecosystem. Thank you for sharing with me, Nick.

You’re welcome, Linda. Enjoy your stay here and have fun exploring our beautiful underwater world!
