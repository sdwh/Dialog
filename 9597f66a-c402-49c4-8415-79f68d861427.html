Hi there! I'm a food blogger and I'm excited to explore the food culture here in Chaozhou. Do you have any recommendations for where to start?

Yes, welcome to Chaozhou! There are plenty of delicious options to choose from, but I highly recommend trying the local specialty of Teochew-style braised goose.

That sounds amazing! Can you recommend a good restaurant for this dish?

Absolutely, one of the best places to try it is at Lao Chen's Braised Goose. The goose is cooked to perfection and the seasoning is just right.

That sounds great! I can't wait to try it. I'm also curious about the local snacks. What kinds of snacks do you recommend I should try?

You definitely have to try the Chaozhou-style oyster omelet and the shrimp dumplings. They're both very popular here and for good reason – they're absolutely delicious.

Yum, those both sound amazing. Is there anything else I should try while I'm here?

You might also want to try the local specialty of Chaozhou-style hotpot. It's a fun and interactive way of eating and there are plenty of different ingredients to choose from.

Sounds like a fun experience! I'm looking forward to it. Are there any must-see sights in the area that I shouldn't miss?

Definitely check out the Kaiyuan Temple – it's a historic landmark and one of the most popular tourist attractions in Chaozhou.
