Hi, thanks for meeting with me today. I'm excited to discuss my new book.

Of course, I can't wait to hear more about it. What's the title?

It's called "Love in the Time of Fried Chicken."

Wow, that's a catchy title. Can you give me a synopsis?

Sure! It's about two young lovers who meet at a fast-food chain and try to make their relationship work despite their different backgrounds.

Sounds like a sweet and engaging story. Who's your target audience?

I'm hoping to appeal to a wide range of readers, but mainly those who enjoy contemporary romance novels.

Have you considered any unique marketing strategies?

Yes, I'm planning on hosting a book signing event at a local fried chicken restaurant. I also created a hashtag for readers to share their favorite fried chicken stories online.

That's brilliant! Any challenges you faced while writing?

Definitely. I had to do a lot of research on fried chicken and fast-food chains. And, of course, my waistline suffered a bit during the process.

Haha, I can imagine. Well, I'm eager to start working on the publication process for your book.

Great, thank you so much for your support. I'm thrilled to bring this story to life for readers.
