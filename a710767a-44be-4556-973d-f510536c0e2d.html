Of course! Kiyomizu-dera is one of the most famous and popular temples in Kyoto. It was founded in 778 and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

One of the main features is the wooden stage overlooking a beautiful view of Kyoto. It's also known for the Otowa waterfall, where visitors can drink from one of three streams for health, longevity or success in studies.

Kiyomizu means "pure water," which refers to the waterfall's pure and clean water.
