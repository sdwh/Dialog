The Opera House has been open since 1973. It took 14 years to build and cost around $102 million AUD.

Wow, that’s really interesting. What is the most popular show that has ever been performed here?

I’d say that the most popular show would have to be ‘The Phantom of the Opera’. It’s an all-time classic and has been performed over 1,900 times at the Opera House.

That’s amazing! How many people can the Sydney Opera House seat?

The Opera House has five main performance venues with a total capacity of around 5,700 people. And the smallest venue can seat up to 400 people.

Of course! The Opera House was designed by Danish architect Jorn Utzon. The building's design is meant to look like a ship sailing on the water, or even a set of sails. It’s definitely a unique piece of architecture.
