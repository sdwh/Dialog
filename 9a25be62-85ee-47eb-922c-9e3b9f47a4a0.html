Wow, that sounds delicious. How about historical and cultural places to visit?

For history and culture, the National Palace Museum and Taipei 101 are great places to start. The National Palace Museum houses some of the most valuable Chinese historical artifacts, while Taipei 101 is one of the tallest buildings in the world.

Yes, there are many great parks to visit, such as Da'an Forest Park and Yangmingshan National Park. They are great places to take a hike and enjoy the natural scenery.
